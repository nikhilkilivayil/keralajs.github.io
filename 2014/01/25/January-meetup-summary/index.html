January meetup summary

2014-01-25

We had our third meetup of KeralaJS on 26th January 2014 at Cochin HackerSpace. There were 14 attendees, including 5 students.

This time we had mainly 2 sessions. Praseed Pai talked about “Comparative analysis of conventional programming languages and JavaScript” where he discussed about the difference between JavaScript and other languages and also the concept prototypical inheritance. Then we took a break for networking and came back for another talk on Setup Node.js development environment and debugging by Revath S Kumar.

Then was a really interesting flash talk on Android Accelerometer controlled bot via WebSockets by Praveen Sridhar.

It was a refreshing experience and we want to keep it going. See you all on next meetup!
